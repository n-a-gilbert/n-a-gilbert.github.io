Our mission is to quantify anthropogenic influences such as habitat loss, climate change, and sensory pollution on wildlife behaviors, populations, and communities. We dissect large datasets with statistical models and emphasize compassionate communication of data and modeling. Most of our work is with birds and mammals, but we enjoy branching out into new systems and taxa. We are based in the Department of Biology at Oklahoma State University.
